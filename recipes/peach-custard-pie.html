Peach Custard Pie

Ingredients

  • Pie crust shell
  • Ripe peaches

Steps

  1. To blind bake the crust
  2. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for about 15 minutes
  4. Make custard
  5. Arrange 12 peach slices on the bottom of the crust
  6. Bake in the preheated oven at 175 degrees C
  7. Let cool completely before serving
